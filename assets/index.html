Empowering Young Entrepreneurs

YouthYC's mission is to empower young aspiring entrepreneurs to learn by building real startups – providing them with the mentorship, resources, and community to turn their ideas into impactful products, while we handle the complexities of company formation and support.

Join as Student Become a Mentor

What We Do

Expert Mentorship

Connect with experienced entrepreneurs who guide you through every step of building your startup, from ideation to launch.

Comprehensive Resources

Access to development tools, business templates, legal guidance, and funding connections to bring your ideas to life.

Supportive Community

Join a network of like-minded young entrepreneurs who share ideas, celebrate wins, and learn from challenges together.

Our Program

Transform Your Ideas Into Reality

YouthYC provides a structured 12-week program where young entrepreneurs learn by doing. We handle company formation, legal complexities, and provide initial funding so you can focus on building great products.

Company Formation

We handle all the legal and administrative work to establish your startup properly.

1-on-1 Mentorship

Weekly sessions with industry experts who've built successful companies.

Initial Funding

$10,000 seed funding to help cover initial development and operational costs.

Product Development

Learn modern development practices and launch your MVP to real users.
